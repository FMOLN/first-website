FMOLN Policies

Delivery & Shipping

Delivery is Free within Addis Ababa. Our courier will contact you to confirm a drop-off time. Payment is collected upon receipt of the item.

We can ship to major regional cities (e.g., Hawassa, Shashemene) with shipping costs confirmed before the item is dispatched. Regional delivery fees are paid upfront.

Return Policy

We accept returns only if the item is in its original, unopened, and flawless condition. Return requests must be made immediately before dispatch or upon delivery if the courier is present.

We cannot accept returns for unpacked, used, or damaged goods. This ensures every customer receives a guaranteed 100% authentic and pristine product.

Terms & Conditions

By placing an order, you agree to the FMOLN Delivery and Return policies outlined above.

All sales are considered final once the product packaging has been opened.
